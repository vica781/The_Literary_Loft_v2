Our Story

Welcome to The Literary Loft, your go-to destination for a curated selection of books.
Our story is one of passion, resilience, and family, born out of a challenging yet transformative time.

Our Beginnings

The idea for The Literary Loft was sparked during the height of the global pandemic. As the world came to a standstill and many of us found solace in the pages of books, it became clear that there was a need for a personalized, online bookshop that could deliver more than just books – it could deliver comfort, adventure, and a sense of community.

From the cozy confines of our living room, my youngest son Timothy and I began to brainstorm. Timothy, a dedicated student with a love for technology, brought fresh, innovative ideas to the table. Together, we envisioned a platform where book lovers could easily find their next great read, interact with fellow bibliophiles, and enjoy a seamless shopping experience.

Building the Dream

With a clear vision in mind, we reached out to my elder son Ilya and his wife Olivia. Ilya's business acumen and Olivia's marketing expertise were invaluable as we started to turn our dream into reality. They brought structure and strategy to our initial ideas, helping us lay a solid foundation for our business.

We worked tirelessly, often late into the night, turning our living room into a hub of creativity and hard work. Timothy managed the technical aspects, building and refining our website, while Ilya focused on business planning and logistics. Olivia, with her keen eye for design, created our brand identity and marketing campaigns.

Growing Together

What began as a family project soon attracted the attention of our friends and extended family, who were eager to contribute. Our small team grew, bringing in diverse skills and perspectives. Each person added their unique touch, from curating book selections to enhancing customer service and expanding our online presence.

Our Mission

At The Literary Loft, we are dedicated to creating a haven for book lovers. Our mission is to provide a curated collection of books that cater to diverse tastes and interests, while fostering a community of readers who share a love for literature.

Our Values

  • Passion for Books: We believe in the transformative power of reading. Every book in our collection is handpicked to ensure it meets our high standards.
  • Family and Community: Our business is built on the strength of family and the support of our community. We strive to create a welcoming space for all book enthusiasts.
  • Quality and Service: We are committed to providing high-quality products and exceptional customer service. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to environmentally friendly practices, using eco-friendly packaging and supporting sustainable publishing initiatives.

Why Your Purchase Matters

Recycling Pre-Loved Books

Each year, approximately 320 million books end up in landfills worldwide, contributing to unnecessary waste. At 'The Literary Loft', we believe that reducing this number is crucial to lowering carbon emissions. Our mission is to make a difference through the three R's: Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle.

By purchasing a pre-loved book, you're playing a vital role in helping the planet—one book at a time. Your choice helps keep books in circulation and out of landfills, ensuring their stories continue to be shared without harming the environment.

Thank you for being a part of the change! Together, we're building a more sustainable future for generations to come.

Supporting Small Business

At "The Literary Loft", we're not just a business—we're a small team of avid book lovers with a passion for stories and a commitment to our community. Every book we sell is wrapped with care, and every order helps us employ local people with big hearts.

When you choose to support small, independent retailers like ours, you're not just buying a book. You're contributing to the diverse and vibrant community of independent booksellers who work hard to keep the love of reading alive.

Thank you for helping us continue sharing stories and supporting local jobs—one book at a time.

Building a Sustainable Future

At "The Literary Loft", we are committed to sustainability. All of our packaging materials are made from recycled materials and can be recycled again and again, ensuring a minimal impact on the environment.

Through our Book Recycling Scheme, you can easily recycle your unwanted books with us. Together, we can reduce waste and help protect the planet, one book at a time.

Thank you for being a part of our journey toward a greener, more sustainable future.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will my order arrive?

All orders are processed and despatched within 1 business day. We offer FREE UK delivery as standard service which takes approximately 3-5 business days to arrive. International parcels typically take between 5-8 business days to arrive. See here for more info on Shipping.

Do you offer speedy delivery?

Yes we do! Delivery for UK orders costs £1.99 and is delivered in 1-2 business days.

What condition can I expect the item to be in?

We sell both pre loved and new items. You will see the item condition on the description page summary. All our pre loved items undergo a 3 stage quality control process by our dedicated warehouse team to ensure that the item is in good condition. All our new items are supplied directly by publishers and will be in pristine condition.

What happens if I receive a damaged item?

No sweat, our excellent customer care team are on hand 5 days a week to resolve any issue and will be happy to organize a replacement for you (where available).

Can I change the delivery address after purchase?

Yes simply shoot us a message on the contact us page and we will endeavour to change the delivery address if it has not been sent out yet.

Can I recycle books with you?

Yes we have a recycling scheme and offer a drop box outside our warehouse in Leicestershire. Just send us a message on our contact us page and we will get back to you.

Do you sell eBooks?

Yes, we will soon be adding eBooks to our store.
